Growth Operating System

The Growth Operating System is a way of thinking that puts the growth of your company at the core of your thinking and organisation.

Adopting the Growth OS way of thinking will be of interest to companies, both new and traditional, who are interested in discovering how to unlock their growth.

The Growth OS is a holistic way of thinking. We combine knowledge of how organisations work, how applying data and technology can drive growth, and the psychology of high performing growth teams. We're describing a rich and deep knowledge, without which growth is impossible.

Your growth team

How to get a team of people to think about growth. Developing a psychologically safe space, where everyone can contribute to your growth. The different roles required within the team.

Measuring growth

How to really do A/B testing well. Measure what really matters. Find your north star metric. Use data to really understand your company's growth.

The Growth Mindset

What individual characteristics make someone great at growth? How can you recruit these individuals. How to create the environment that helps these individuals contribute and grow together.

Practical knowledge

The Growth OS is practical knowledge, based on our experience. This isn't a theory, this knowledge is something that you can take into your organisation and start applying straight away.

Our Course

We have developed a workshop format course. Over a two day period we will take you through the different components of the Growth OS. Our course doesn't just contain lots of theoretical knowledge. We have also created an array of hands-on practical examples and tools. This isn't a dry lecture, you'll get to learn by doing.
